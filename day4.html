    We visited T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117 to learn about emergency response operations.

    This visit emphasized how communication systems and coordination are critical in saving lives, highlighting another important application of IT in public service.

  • "Day 4 – November 15 | Loboc River Cruise"

    We enjoyed a fiesta buffet lunch aboard the floating restaurant while cruising the Loboc River, accompanied by live Harana performances.

    Journey Reflection

    The cruise blended food, music, and scenery, offering a memorable cultural experience that highlighted Bohol’s charm.

  • "Day 4 – November 15 | Bohol Heritage & Sightseeing"

    These stops showcased Bohol’s culture and natural beauty, giving us lasting memories of history, nature, and local pride.
